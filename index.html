Toolbox Focus

Built for classroom use, not just a list of links.

Math/STEM Instruction

Tools that help students explore math, science, coding, simulations, and problem-solving through visual and interactive learning.

Assessment

Formative checks, review activities, and response tools that give teachers timely evidence of student understanding.

Creativity

Media, design, presentation, and production tools that support student expression and teacher-created learning materials.

Collaboration

Shared workspaces and productivity tools that help students and teachers plan, organize, and build ideas together.

Communication

Classroom management and communication tools that support clear expectations, family contact, and student access to information.

AI

Teacher productivity tools that can support planning, feedback, differentiation, and reflection when used thoughtfully and ethically.

Classroom Connection

Organized around purposeful technology use.

  1. Each tool is connected to a classroom purpose rather than listed as a standalone link.
  2. Tool cards include classroom uses, teacher value, artifacts, and standards connections.
  3. Module reflections document how course ideas connect to instructional practice.
  4. Resources support ethical, standards-aligned decisions about educational technology.
