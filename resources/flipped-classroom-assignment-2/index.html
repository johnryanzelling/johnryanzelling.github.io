Lesson Overview

This resource is a student-facing shell for a flipped seventh-grade pre-algebra lesson. Students preview the Pythagorean theorem before class, arrive ready for targeted support, and spend class time applying the idea through collaborative puzzles.

  • Topic: Pythagorean theorem foundations
  • Audience: Regular Grade 7 Mathematics students who need remedial bridge support
  • Sequence: Watch, note, check readiness, practice, collaborate, reflect
