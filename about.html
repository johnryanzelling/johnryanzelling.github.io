Educator Background

I am an educator developing a practical technology toolbox for use in mathematics and related classroom settings. My goal is to select tools that support conceptual understanding, student engagement, creativity, collaboration, and meaningful assessment.

Technology Philosophy

Technology is most valuable when it deepens learning rather than simply adding novelty. The tools in this portfolio are evaluated for instructional purpose, accessibility, teacher workflow, student agency, and alignment with ISTE standards.

Purpose

The EDUC 675 Digital Toolbox organizes 21 teacher tools with descriptions, classroom-use ideas, pros and cons, ISTE connections, examples, and resource links.
