Formula Reference

a2 + b2 = c2

The legs are a and b. The hypotenuse is c, across from the right angle.

Lock 1: Identify the Hypotenuse

Record the side opposite the right angle for each triangle.

Lock 2: Squares and Square Roots

Match each side length with its square area and reverse the relationship.

Lock 3: Find the Missing Hypotenuse

For each triangle, show substitution, squaring, addition, and the positive square root.

Lock 4: Determine Whether It Is a Right Triangle

Compare a2 + b2 with c2. Record the comparison and decision.

Final Challenge: Real-World Distances

Record the three real-world distances and use them to form the completion code.
