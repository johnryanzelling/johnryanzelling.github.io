What Makes a Right Triangle?

  • The right angle is the square corner.
  • The legs meet at the right angle.
  • The hypotenuse is opposite the right angle.

Quick Review: Squares and Square Roots

32 = 9

42 = 16

√25 = 5

Speaking purpose: remind students that squaring finds area and square roots move back to side length.

The Area Idea

The square on side a plus the square on side b matches the square on side c.

The Formula

a2 + b2 = c2

  • a and b are the legs.
  • c is the hypotenuse.
  • c is always opposite the right angle.

Worked Example: 3-4-5 Triangle

32 + 42 = c2

9 + 16 = c2

25 = c2

c = 5
